About

Every single year, it is estimated that more than 300 million people suffer from depression, with Kenya ranking 6th in Africa with 1.9 million people in 2017.

Unfortunately, medical care for those affected is not only expensive, but not easily accessible. Also, it tends to affect the youth mostly, who are just finding their feet economically. Add to that fact the stigma associated with mental health issues and most people feel like it's hopeless, leading them to measures like comitting suicide.

Thus when we created the bot (that we hope you enjoy using) we had a few simple missions:
To give someone suffering hope that they can get better
To lessen the stigma around mental health by starting conversations about it
To enable people get access to the many institutions that will give them the care they deserve
To reassure them that they are worthy

Frequently asked questions

What is the app?

It is a knowledge based system specializing in mental health issues.

What is a knowledge based system?

A knowledge-based system(KBS) is a computer-based system that uses and generates knowledge from data, information and knowledge, acting as an expert on-demand anytime, anywhere. (Akerkar & Sajja, 2010).

Who will it serve?

It will serve the youth of the country, aged between 13-25. They will be divided based on their age since their experiences and thus their problems change with age.

Why not everyone?

Because this is an evolving product and we will try and add the functionality to aid as many people as possible later.

Any other restrictions?

Yes, unfortunately it only works in English,so please bear with us as we try to add other languages.

Any extra skills I need to interact with it?

Nope, just an understanding of English.
